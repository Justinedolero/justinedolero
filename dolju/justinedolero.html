About Bangus Business


Bangus, also known as milkfish, is a versatile and popular fish in Asian cuisine. Recognized for its tender, boneless flesh and mild flavor, bangus is often grilled, fried, or smoked. This species thrives in both salt and freshwater environments, making it a vital part of aquaculture. Rich in omega-3 fatty acids, vitamins, and minerals, bangus is not only delicious but also a nutritious addition to meals. Its popularity extends to various Filipino dishes, such as "sinigang," "bangus sisig," and "bangus belly adobo." As a sustainable seafood choice, bangus plays a significant role in culinary traditions and aquaculture practices.
